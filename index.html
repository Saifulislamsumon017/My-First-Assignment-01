Bangladesh 2.0

Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

The act of introducing new ideas, methods, or products to drive progress, solve challenges, and create value.

Sustainability

The practice of meeting present needs without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own, focusing on environmental, social, and economic balance.

Infrastructure

The fundamental systems and structures, such as transportation, communication, energy, and utilities, that support the functioning and development of a society or organization.

Empowerment

The process of enabling individuals or groups to gain confidence, skills, and authority to make decisions and take control of their lives or work.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Energy generated from renewable and natural sources like sunlight, wind, water, and biomass, and supports sustainability.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
